In a hash table of tableSize slots, what is the smallest number of records that must be inserted before the probability of a collision reaches percentage%?

theAnswer

The formula maybe help: \(1 - \frac{t!}{(t-n)!(t^n)}\)
\(t\) is the table size and \(n\) is the number of records inserted.
