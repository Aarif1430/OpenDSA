Instructions:

For this exercise you will trace the KMP algorithm in its entirity. You will start by highlighting the index from both the master string and substring that is currently being compared (simply click the index you want). Then you can click the Next button to move onto the next step. When you have found a match or made it to the end of the master string (whenever you think the algorithm has ended) you can click the Grade button. The alignment array has been precomputed for your convenience.
