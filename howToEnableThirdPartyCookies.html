We have included instructions to enable Third Party Cookies in various browsers below.

Google Chrome:

  1. Open Settings menu.
  2. Click on 'Show advanced settings...' link and scroll down to 'Privacy'.
  3. Click on the 'Content settings...' button.
  4. Under 'Cookies' click 'Manage exceptions' button.
  5. Add (https://lti.cs.vt.edu) as new 'Hostname pattern' and select 'Allow' Behavior.
